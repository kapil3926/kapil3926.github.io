Financial Services Mobile App

Tech Stack: React Native, TypeScript, and GraphQL
My role. Technical Project Lead
Project Description:

The client sought to develop a mobile banking application to offer a seamless and secure banking experience to its customers.

Solution: Leveraging my expertise in React Native, TypeScript, and GraphQL, I developed a feature-rich mobile banking application that provided customers with access to account balances, transaction history, fund transfers, and bill payments. Security measures, including biometric authentication and encryption, were implemented to ensure data privacy.
